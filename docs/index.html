about

An AI-powered extension that automatically blurs potentially triggering words/segments of articles.

our inspiration

Content on news or entertainment related sites/forums often contains disturbing or triggering language seemingly out of nowhere

the mission

Users should be able to scroll through their favorite news/social media site without having to worry about seeing potentially disturbing/triggering content
